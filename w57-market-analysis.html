04.

Target Market

Primary Target

Vietnamese diaspora & Asian Americans

Demographics: Ages 25-45, urban/suburban

Psychographics: Values authenticity, cultural connection, premium quality without extravagance

Behaviors: Active on social media, seeks convenience, willing to pay for heritage products

Secondary Target

Specialty coffee drinkers seeking convenience

Profile: Quality-conscious consumers who want premium instant coffee for on-the-go

Pain Points: Limited time, wants quality without brewing complexity

Opportunity: Growing segment (+18% specialty coffee adoption since 2020)

Key Insight

Authentic heritage + cultural connection = powerful differentiation The Vietnamese diaspora market is underserved in the premium instant coffee space. W57 combines: • Cultural authenticity (Vietnamese recipe, heritage story) • Premium quality (freeze-dried, Himalayan salt, organic sugar) • Accessible pricing ($0.75-1.25 vs. $1.67-3.00+ ultra-premium) • Convenience (instant, portable, no brewing needed) This positioning creates a unique market opportunity.
05.

Go-to-Market Roadmap

5-Phase Launch Strategy

Phase 1: Amazon Launch (0-6 months)

Objective: Establish product-market fit, generate initial reviews

Activities: Amazon FBA setup, product listing optimization, initial PPC campaigns, review generation strategy

Success Metrics: 100-250 reviews, 35-40% gross margin, $10K-30K monthly revenue

Phase 2: DTC Website (6-12 months)

Objective: Capture higher margins, build brand, develop direct customer relationships

Activities: Shopify store setup, SEO optimization, email marketing, social media presence

Success Metrics: 55-65% gross margin, 20-30% repeat purchase rate, $30K-80K monthly revenue

Phase 3: Retail Expansion (12-24 months)

Objective: Increase distribution, brand awareness, volume

Activities: Asian grocery stores, specialty food retailers, local cafes, farmer's markets

Success Metrics: 10-20 retail accounts, 40-50% gross margin (wholesale), $100K-300K monthly revenue

Phase 4: Subscription Model (24+ months)

Objective: Predictable revenue, customer retention, lifetime value

Activities: Monthly/quarterly subscription offering, loyalty programs, personalized bundles

Success Metrics: 15-25% subscription rate, 70%+ retention, $200K-500K monthly revenue

Phase 5: Wholesale Partnerships (36+ months)

Objective: Scale through partnerships, maximize reach

Activities: National retail chains, hospitality partnerships, corporate accounts

Success Metrics: National distribution, $500K-1M+ monthly revenue
