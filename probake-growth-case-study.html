  • Pause if: CTR < 2% after 1,000 impressions OR cost per lead > $400

  • Kill if: No conversions after $500 spend OR sales team rejects all leads

Contingency Planning: What If Things Don't Go as Expected?

Scenario 1: Leads Below Target

Problem: Month 1 delivers 10 leads instead of 15-20

Action Plan:

  • Week 1-2: Increase Google Ads budget by 50% ($4K ‚Üí $6K)
  • Week 2-3: Launch additional keyword groups (broaden targeting)
  • Week 3-4: Add LinkedIn retargeting for website visitors
  • Alternative: Extend timeline to Month 2 for optimization, accept lower volume in Month 1

Scenario 2: High Cost Per Lead

Problem: CPL is $350-400 instead of $200-250

Action Plan:

  • Option A (Reduce Volume): Accept 25-30 leads/month instead of 40-50, stay within budget
  • Option B (Improve Conversion): Optimize landing pages to convert 8-10% instead of 6%
  • Option C (Lower Bids): Reduce Google Ads bidding strategy, accept longer ramp time

ROI Still Works: Even at $400 CPL, if close rate is 10% and avg deal is $100K, that's $10K revenue per lead = 25x ROI

Scenario 3: Low Lead Quality

Problem: Leads are unqualified (students, tire-kickers, wrong geography)

Action Plan:

  • Immediate: Add negative keywords ("free", "cheap", "used", "repair")
  • Week 2: Tighten geographic targeting (exclude outside Eastern US)
  • Week 3: Add qualification questions to forms (budget, timeline, decision authority)
  • Week 4: Implement lead scoring in HubSpot (auto-flag low-quality leads)

Scenario 4: Long Sales Cycle Delays Results

Problem: Leads take 9-12 months to close, not 3-6 months

Action Plan:

  • Measurement Shift: Track leading indicators (pipeline value, sales conversations, quote requests) not just closed revenue
  • Expectation Setting: Communicate to stakeholders that Month 1-6 builds pipeline, closed revenue comes Month 6-12
  • Quick Wins: Focus on smaller deal sizes ($50K-75K range) that close faster to show early wins

Scenario 5: Budget Constraints During Transition

Problem: Business hesitant to commit full $10K/month during ownership transition

Action Plan:

  • Pilot Approach: Start with $5K/month (Google Ads only) for 2 months
  • Milestone-Based: Hit 15 leads in Month 1 ‚Üí unlock full $10K budget for Month 2
  • Performance Guarantee: If CPL > $400 in Month 1, reduce budget in Month 2
