Bello Growth Case Study: From Startup to Standout

Client Profile

  • Client: Bello Water
  • Industry: Water Filtration
  • Timeline: October 25 – December 31, 2024
  • Location: Toronto, Canada

When I joined Bello Water on October 25, 2024, the company was struggling to establish itself as a professional brand. With monthly revenue stagnating at $4K–$7K, Bello's digital presence felt disjointed and unpolished—more like a bootstrapped startup than the innovative water filtration company it aspired to be. Over the next six weeks, I led a creative and strategic overhaul of their website UX, Meta ads, and email marketing flows. By December 31, 2024, Bello had generated $160K in incremental Q4 sales and emerged as a polished, trustworthy brand.

This case study unpacks the three core strategies—UX Overhaul, Klaviyo Automations, and Meta Ads—along with a quick-win Capsule Sale, to show how creativity and data-driven precision can transform a business.

Executive Summary

This case study details how a focused six-week strategy—including a UX overhaul, Klaviyo email automation, Meta Ads optimization, and a quick-win capsule sale—generated $160K in incremental Q4 revenue and rebranded Bello Water as a polished, industry-leading brand.

1. UX Overhaul: From Clunky to Compelling

Challenge

Bello's website was a conversion killer: a 65% bounce rate, 50-second average sessions, and a dismal 1.15% conversion rate. It looked and felt like a startup scrambling to get by—not the innovative water filtration company it aspired to be.

Action

I dove into a creative redesign process, focusing on both aesthetics and functionality:

  • Simplified Navigation: Reorganized the menu and content structure for intuitive browsing.
  • Engaging Visuals: Replaced the static homepage banner with a 10-second product demo video that instantly communicated value.
  • Bold CTAs and Social Proof: Added two-column content blocks with clear calls-to-action and customer testimonials to build trust.

Result

  • Bounce rate dropped 25% to 48.75%.
  • Session duration increased 30% to 65 seconds.
  • Conversion rate jumped from 1.15% to 3.2%, contributing ~$8K to Q4 revenue.

2. Email Automation: Crafting a Polished Customer Journey

Challenge

With a 70% cart abandonment rate and no strategy to re-engage lapsed users, Bello was leaving money on the table.

Action

I designed a series of visually stunning, mobile-optimized email flows using Klaviyo, each tailored to reflect Bello's new professional image:

  • Abandoned Checkout: A three-email sequence with escalating urgency and a final discount offer.
  • Added to Cart: Personalized recommendations and a clear CTA to complete the purchase.
  • Browse Abandon Flow: Short product videos tied to viewed items to re-engage browsers.
  • Post-Purchase Flows: Thank-you messages, order updates, and referral prompts to foster loyalty.
  • Replenishment and Win-Back: Data-driven emails with personalized incentives and new product highlights.
  • Welcome Flow: A polished video introduction to Bello's brand with a first-purchase incentive.

Result

  • Flows drove 18% of total revenue (~$10K in Q4), with the Post-Purchase flow alone generating $4K.
  • Klaviyo delivered a 3.5× ROI.

Quick Win: Capsule Sale

Challenge

Bello needed an immediate revenue boost for a new capsule product launch.

Action

I launched a targeted, time-sensitive campaign:

  • VIP Email: Exclusive early access to the top 10% of customers by lifetime value.
  • Sticky Banner: A homepage countdown timer to create urgency.
  • Social Media Blitz: Quick-turnaround posts across Bello's channels.

Result

  • Generated $10K in two days, up from a $200 daily average.

3. Meta Ads: Recycling with a Sexy, Professional Edge

Challenge

Bello's Meta ads were tied to an old Kickstarter campaign with a $1 entry point, a 1.15% conversion rate, and a ROAS of just 1.5×. They screamed "bootstrapped startup."

Action

With no time to waste, I transformed their old assets into professional, high-performing ads:

  • Asset Revamp: Re-edited videos with polished transitions and vibrant visuals; redesigned static ads with consistent branding.
  • Pixel Fix: Installed and QA'd the Meta Pixel site-wide.
  • Lookalike Audience: Built a 1% LAL from top LTV customers.
  • Funnel Split: Separated Prospecting and Retargeting campaigns for better optimization.

Result

  • Conversion rate soared to 3.2%.
  • ROAS improved from 1.5× to 2.8×.
  • Conversions grew 22%, adding ~$52K to Q4 revenue.
