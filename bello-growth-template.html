Bello Growth Case Study: From Startup to Standout

October 25 – December 31, 2024 · Industry: Water Filtration · Montreal

When I joined Bello Water, the brand felt like a bootstrapped startup—$4K–$7K monthly revenue and a disjointed digital presence. In six weeks we rebuilt the growth engine through a UX overhaul, Klaviyo automations, Meta Ads, and a focused capsule sale—driving $160K in incremental Q4 sales.

01.

UX Overhaul: From Clunky to Compelling

UX Overhaul

Design / English
2024
Website redesign
Case study notes

Challenge

65% bounce, 50s sessions, and a 1.15% conversion rate held back growth.

Action

  • Simplified navigation and information architecture.
  • Replaced static banner with a 10s product demo video.
  • Introduced two-column blocks, bold CTAs, and social proof.

Result

  • Bounce ↓ 25%
  • Session ↑ 30%
  • CR ↑ to 3.2% (~$8K Q4)

Email Automation: Crafting a Polished Customer Journey

Email Automation

Klaviyo / English
2024
Flows library
Case study notes

Challenge

70% cart abandonment and silent lapsed users.

Action

  • Abandoned Checkout, Added to Cart, Browse Abandon flows.
  • Post-Purchase with referrals; Replenishment and Win‑Back.
  • Welcome flow with brand video and first‑purchase incentive.

Result

  • Flows = 18% of revenue (~$10K Q4)
  • 3.5× ROI on Klaviyo spend

Meta Ads: Recycling with a Sexy, Professional Edge

Meta Ads

Acquisition / English
2024
Campaign structure
Case study notes

Challenge

Legacy Kickstarter assets; ROAS 1.5×; conversion 1.15%.

Action

  • Re‑edited video and static assets with consistent branding.
  • Installed and QA’d Meta Pixel; built 1% LAL from top LTV.
  • Split Prospecting vs Retargeting funnels.

Result

  • ROAS ↑ to 2.8×
  • CPA ↓ 35% ($35 → $23)
  • Conversions ↑ 22% (~$52K Q4)
